KNOW THE DAY YOUR BORN

Normally we know the date we were born but it is hard for us to remember the day whether it was on a monday or a friday or any other day.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a day name which corresponds to the day in the week as they were born.By knowing the day your were born you can also get to know you Akan name.
